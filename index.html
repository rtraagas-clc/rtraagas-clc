ABOUT UP IN THE CLOUDS
What is Up in the Clouds about?

Up in the Clouds is a contemporary satirical essay that highlights the errors of homophobia and shows the beauty of individuality. It's set in the fictional fantasy world of Skytown where creatures called Skyfolk are born with black-and-white patterned skin. When a sudden influx of individuals of different-patterned skin are born, chaos ensues and everyone is trying to put a stop deviation from the norm.

This is a heartfelt story that teaches us to dare to be different, and that not all things are black and white.
Yael Raagas.☁️
